    Learn About the Planets in our Solar System

    Planet Facts

    Mercury

    • ★ Mercury is about 36.04 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ Mercury's radius is about 1,289 miles.
    • ★ One day on Mercury is equal to almost 59 Earth days! This is equal to 1,408 hours.
    • ★ Mercury is mainly made up of iron. This planet is terrestrial which means it has a solid surface made of silicate rocks or metals.
    • ★ Mercury has no moons.

    Venus

    • ★ Venus is about 36.04 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ Mercury's radius is about 1,289 miles.
    • ★ One day on Mercury is equal to almost 59 Earth days! This is equal to 1,408 hours.
    • ★ Mercury is mainly made up of iron. This planet is terrestrial which means it has a solid surface made of silicate rocks or metals.
    • ★ Mercury has no moons.

    Earth

    • ★ Earth is about 92.96 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ Earth's radius is approximately 3,963 miles.
    • ★ One day on Earth is equal to 24 hours.
    • ★ Earth has three layers, the crust, the mantle and the core. The core consists primarily of iron and nickel and the outside layer of Earth is made of solid rock.
    • ★ Earth has one moon.

    Mars

    • ★ Mars is about 141.6 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ The radius of Mars is 2,106.1 miles.
    • ★ The day length of Mars is very similar to Earth. A day on Mars lasts 24 hours, 37 minutes and 22 seconds.
    • ★ Mars is made of iron, nickel, and sulfur. Surrounding the core there is a rocky mantle, followed by the crust which is made of iron, magnesium, aluminum, calcium, and potassium.
    • ★ Mars has two moons.

    Jupiter

    • ★ Jupiter is about 483.8 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ The radius of Jupiter is about 43,441 miles.
    • ★ One day on Jupiter only takes about 10 hours. That means a day on Jupiter is not even half a day on Earth.
    • ★ Jupiter has a composition similar to the Sun. It is mostly made of hydrogen and helium. Jupiter also has the larget ocean in the solar system, however the ocean is made of hydrogen instead of water. Jupiter's rings are primarily made of dust.
    • ★ Jupiter has 95 moons.

    Saturn

    • ★ Saturn is about 890.8 million miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ The radius of Saturn is about 36,184 miles.
    • ★ A day on Saturn is very similar to a day on Jupiter. One day is equal to about 10 hours and 34 minutes.
    • ★ Saturn is a gas planet mostly made up of hydrogen and helium. However, at Saturn's center there is a dense core made of iron and nickel. Saturn also has rings made of billions of pieces of ice, dust and rocks.
    • ★ Saturn has 146 moons.

    Uranus

    • ★ Uranus is about 1.784 billion miles away from the Sun!
    • ★ The radius of Uranus is about 15,179 miles.
    • ★ One day on Uranus is equal to 17.24 Earth hours.
    • ★ Uranus is an ice giant which is mostly made up of a hot dense fluid of icy materials like water, methane, and ammonia. The core of Uranus is rocky and can heat up to 9,000 degrees Fahrenheit! Uranus also has rings made up of icy chunks.
    • ★ Uranus has 28 moons.

    Neptune

    • ★ Neptune is about 2.793 billion miles from the Sun!
    • ★ The radius of Neptune is about 15,299 miles.
    • ★ One day on Neptune is equal to about 16 Earth hours.
    • ★ Neptune is another ice giant made up of icy materials like water, methane, and ammonia. Neptune also has a small, rocky core. Neptune's rings are made up of small rocks and dust.
    • ★ Neptune has 14 moons.
